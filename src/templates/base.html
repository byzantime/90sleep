About Sleep Cycles

Sleep occurs in cycles of approximately 90 minutes. Each cycle consists of different stages, from light sleep to deep sleep and REM sleep.

Waking up between cycles (rather than during a cycle) helps you feel more refreshed and alert. This calculator accounts for the average 14 minutes it takes to fall asleep.
